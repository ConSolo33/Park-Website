About

Nestled in a valley just west of downtown Evergreen, Evergreen Lake inspires feelings of solace and a connection with nature while still being easily accesible. Located just 25 minutes West of Denver, Evergreen Lake is the perfect destination for an afternoon getaway. Plentiful walking paths and accessible services provide a no-hassle experience for any visitor. There are also numerous seasonal activities throughout the year, including fishing, boating, paddleboarding, nature walks, local concerts, and ice-skating.
